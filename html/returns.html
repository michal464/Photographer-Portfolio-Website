Returns information

At Yaron Barkai Photography, we are committed to your complete satisfaction with your purchases. If for any reason you are not happy with your purchase, we offer a flexible and convenient return and exchange policy. You may return or exchange items purchased within 30 days of receipt. If the product arrives damaged or broken, please notify us immediately, but no later than 7 days from receipt, so we can address the return or exchange. The product must be in its original condition, without any damages or alterations made by you. Please return the product in its original packaging along with all accompanying accessories. If the product arrives broken or damaged during shipping, please contact us immediately with photos of the damaged product and packaging. We will replace the product with a new one at no additional cost, or refund your money according to your preference. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service at customer.service@yaronbarkaiphoto.com with your order details and reason for return. Once you receive approval from our team, please ship the product back to the address provided. Return shipping costs will be borne by the customer, except in cases where the product arrived damaged or broken. After receiving the product and verifying that it meets the conditions, we will refund your full purchase amount. The refund will be issued using the same payment method you used for the purchase, within 14 business days of receiving the returned product. We understand that sometimes things can go wrong during shipping or you might not be satisfied with the product you ordered. Therefore, we promise to do everything we can to ensure you are happy with your purchase and provide prompt and efficient handling of any return or exchange request. Thank you for choosing Yaron Barkai Photography. We value you as a customer and are here to assist you with any questions or requests. Best regards, The Yaron Barkai Photography Team
